Acquisition Deal

Acquisition Deal is an agreement signed between a Licensor/Assignor and Licensee/Assignee which contains Rights acquired for a title.

This module will be accessible to only those users who have appropriate rights for this module.

To add, modify, delete the data in/from the system will be done by the user who has rights for individual action (add, modify, delete, approve, decline, amendment, clone, close deal)
