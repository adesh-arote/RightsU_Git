Material

To add Material details, click on the 'Add' Button

Select Title for which the given Material will be applicable

Material type can be selected from the drop down

Material Medium can be selected from the drop down

Specify the Quantity of Materials in the Quantity field

Click on the save icon located in the Action column to save the Material

Click on Cancel Button, the Record will not get updated

Click on Edit Button to modify the Record

Click on Delete Button in the Action column to delete the Record
